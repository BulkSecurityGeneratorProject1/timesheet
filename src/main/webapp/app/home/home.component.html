Application Features:

  • The Time Sheet system has been designed in order to track time spent on various projects, assess related costs and ultimately optimize project resource plan.
  • The initial page of Time sheet is always the current week so that the employee can fill the time sheet day by day for the active week.
  • In case the employee wants to fill his time sheet on the following Sunday for the past week, `Previous` and `Next` buttons shall be used to reach the appropriate week.
  • Administrator Roles can create Project and Approve or Reject a timesheet with optional feedback
